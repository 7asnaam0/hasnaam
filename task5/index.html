about me

Corey James is an Australian graphic designer specialising in editorial and visual identity design. He previously worked in New York City with Hubert & Fischer designing catalogues and exhibition materials for artists. He now resides in Melbourne working as a designer for Fabio Ongarato Design and taking on selected freelance projects.
